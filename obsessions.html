From one obsession to the next...


Ever since I was young, I've tended to pursue my interests in waves that I'll call obsessions. I go full bore on them until they peter out and the next one comes along. I did it with stamp collecting and comic books in the 1980s. The stamp one was kind of bad, as I remember getting into some sort of service which sent new stamps each week, and had a tight deadline to return them if you didn't want them, otherwise you had to pay. I remember my Dad having to step in and help me craft a letter to the company to get the service to stop. I think they eventually did but it took a couple of tries.


Today the obsessions thing continues, my latest one being dusting off my coding skills. I'm an engineer, and I feel it's essential I known how to solve problems by writing a computer program to code the solution or build a website to convey knowlege.


I'd like to try to provide information which allows you the reader to derive something of benefit from my obsessions. I'll likely use the blog I started over on Medium for this purpose.


Stay tuned!
