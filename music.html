Alice Cooper


Special Forces


The favourite album of the month is "Special Forces" by Alice Cooper, released in 1981.


Track List:


  • Who Do You Think We Are?
  • Seven & Seven Is
  • Prettiest Cop on the Block
  • Don't Talk Old to Me
  • Generation Landslide '81
  • Skeletons in the Closet
  • You Want It, You Got It
  • You Look Good in Rags
  • You're A Movie
  • Vicious Rumours

I never really was that in to Alice Cooper. I liked a couple of his classics, but never really acquainted myself with his entire catalog. After being pointed at a list of hard rock and heavy metal albums that turned 40 this year (yes, I'm old), "Special Forces" was on the list and has been such a gem. Favourite songs are "Prettiest Cop on the Block", "Skeletons in the Closet" and "You're a Movie".


After hearing this album, I explored some other of Alice's past work, but nothing else has stuck quite like this one. His style, generally, is not my favourite, but this album is an exception. Fortunately the era of cassette tapes is over...if it wasn't, and I had this album on cassette tape, it would be worn out. :)
